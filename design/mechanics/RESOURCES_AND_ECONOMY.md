# Resources & Economy

> Sultan's Game equivalent: [Resource System](../sultans-game-reference/COMPLETE_MECHANICS.md#resource-system)

## Primary Currency: Silver Taels (银两)

The basic currency for purchasing, bribing, and building.

### Generation
| Source | Requirements | Amount |
|---|---|---|
| **Estate management** (daily) | Cunning + Eloquence check | 1-3 silver based on success |
| **Wu family allowance** | Automatic every 7 days | 2 silver (increases with rank) |
| **Event rewards** | Complete certain events | Variable |
| **Trade contacts** (unlockable) | Shadow Reach 3 | Passive income based on Shadow Reach level |
| **Contemplation** (pondering silver) | Once per day | Triggers 1 of 5 income events |
| **Selling equipment/items** | Any time | Half purchase value |

### Spending
| Use | Cost |
|---|---|
| Scrolls at Imperial Library | 1 silver |
| Healing at Palace Physician | 1 silver |
| Building Wu Family Network contact point | 10 silver |
| Training eunuch agents | 5 silver |
| Equipment purchases | 2-10 silver |
| Festival preparation | 5-15 silver |
| Bribes | 1-5 silver |
| Gifts to Emperor | 3-10 silver |
| Gifts to other consorts | 1-5 silver |

## Intelligence Scrolls (情报)

Consumable information cards used as **rerolls** during dice checks.

### Generation
| Source | Details |
|---|---|
| Court victories | Win a political exchange at morning court |
| Imperial Gardens | Eavesdrop successfully (Discretion check) |
| Spy network | Shadow Reach 5+ generates 1 scroll per 7 days |
| Contemplation | Ponder intelligence → triggers info-gathering event |
| Agent storylines | Some characters produce intelligence as part of their arc |

### Types
Each pair of stats has a corresponding intelligence type. Using the matching type gives **+1 bonus die** on the reroll:

| Intelligence Type | Stat Pair | Flavor |
|---|---|---|
| **Court Whispers** (宫闱密语) | Eloquence + Cunning | Overheard conversations, political rumors |
| **Palace Secrets** (宫中秘辛) | Discretion + Resourcefulness | Hidden passages, guard rotations, supply routes |
| **Medical Notes** (医案) | Scholarship + Vitality | Poison symptoms, pregnancy signs, illness patterns |
| **Spiritual Omens** (天兆) | Spiritual Arts + Resolve | Dreams, prophecies, astrological readings |
| **Beauty Techniques** (容术) | Beauty + any | Makeup secrets, fashion intelligence, rival's weaknesses |

## Golden Dice (金骰)

Extremely rare. Each provides 1 automatic success on any check.

### Sources
- Temple blessings (rare, requires high Virtue)
- Extraordinary event rewards
- Wu Ledger meta-progression boons
- Starting allotment based on difficulty

## 5 Reputation Metrics

Reputation metrics are **not currencies** — they are persistent counters that unlock thresholds and affect the world.

### 1. Virtue (贤德)
> Sultan's equivalent: Renown

How the palace sees your moral character.

| Threshold | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 3 | **Buddhist Temple** access — meditation, spiritual events, sanctuary from enemies |
| 5 | **Empress Dowager's notice** — she may intervene on your behalf in emergencies |
| 10 | **Reset edict timer** up to 3 times per run (the Dowager grants extensions) |
| 15 | **"Virtuous Consort" title** — passive Imperial Favor gain, rival consorts hesitate to attack openly |
| 20 | **Break one edict freely** — your virtue alone satisfies the requirement |

**Gained by**: Acts of mercy, Buddhist devotion, self-restraint, scholarship, protecting the weak
**Lost by**: Poisoning, framing, cruelty, deception (if caught)

### 2. Ruthlessness (狠辣)
> Sultan's equivalent: Infamy

Your reputation for cruelty and willingness to destroy.

| Threshold | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 3 | **Rivals take you seriously** — direct confrontations become available |
| 5 | **Poison merchant** access, assassination tools, dark scheme events |
| 10 | **Other consorts plot your downfall** — assassination defense events trigger |
| 15 | **"Poison Consort" title** — agents assigned against you have morale penalties |
| 20 | **Fear rules the palace** — unlocks darkest ending paths (tyrant Empress route) |

**Gained by**: Poisoning, framing rivals, cruel punishments, betraying allies, ruthless schemes
**Lost by**: Acts of genuine mercy (not strategic mercy)

### 3. Imperial Favor (圣眷)
> Sultan's equivalent: Influence

The Emperor's personal regard for you. The most mechanically important metric.

**Effects**:
- Determines rank advancement eligibility
- Protects against accusations (high favor = Emperor dismisses slander)
- Enables special requests (appointments for Wu allies, resource grants)
- Determines frequency of bedchamber visit invitations

**Gained by**: Court victories, gifts to Emperor, successful edict fulfillment, producing an heir
**Lost by**: Slander accumulation (3 Slander = -5 Favor), public disgrace, boring the Emperor

### 4. Shadow Reach (暗网)
> Sultan's equivalent: Notoriety

Your influence in the palace underground — eunuchs, servants, black market.

| Threshold | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 3 | **Wu Family Network** contact point (recruit outside agents) |
| 3 | **Eunuch spy network** (information events) |
| 5 | **Poison merchant**, black market goods |
| 7 | **Trade contacts** passive income |
| 10 | **Chief Eunuch's ear** — can request eunuch intervention in events |

**Gained by**: Eunuch dealings, bribes, black market purchases, spy operations
**Lost by**: Getting caught in illegal activities

### 5. Heavenly Sight (天眼)
> Sultan's equivalent: Insight

Spiritual/supernatural awareness.

| Threshold | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 3 | **Prophetic dreams** — hints about upcoming events |
| 7 | **Temple inner sanctum** access |
| 14 | **Spirit communication** — supernatural event chains |
| 21 | **Divine visions** — unlocks transcendent ending paths |
| 28 | **Enlightenment** — ultimate spiritual ending |

**Gained by**: Meditation, temple visits, spiritual events, Buddhist/Taoist study
**Lost by**: Excessive worldly indulgence, certain ruthless acts

## Slander (谗言)

Negative accumulating resource from court politics.

- Rivals whisper against you at court
- Each Slander is a card that sits in your hand taking up space
- **At 3 Slander**: lose 5 Imperial Favor + "Imperial Rebuke" event (punishment, possible demotion)
- After the rebuke, all 3 Slander cards are destroyed
- You can **preemptively counter** slander through court events, but failure worsens it

## Economy Balance Notes

The economy should feel **tight but not suffocating**:
- Silver should be scarce enough that you can't do everything, forcing prioritization
- Intelligence scrolls should feel precious — using one is a real decision
- Golden Dice should feel like miracles — 1-3 per run maximum
- Reputation metrics should gate content, not be grindable currencies
