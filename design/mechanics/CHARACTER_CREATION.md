# Character Creation & Tutorial Prologue

> Three layers of customization, then a tutorial that teaches mechanics through the palace selection process.
> Experienced players can skip the tutorial and go straight to the build summary.

---

## Overview

```
LAYER 1: Family Background     → base stats, starting silver, family connection
LAYER 2: Education & Upbringing → stat bonuses, knowledge, starting equipment
LAYER 3: Personality & Hobbies  → traits, Chunhua's archetype, starting reputation
         ↓
TUTORIAL: The Four Virtues Selection (skippable)
         → teaches game mechanics through story
         → performance tweaks starting build
         ↓
GAME STARTS (Day 1, you've been in the palace a few weeks)
```

## Tutorial: The Four Virtues Selection (四德选秀)

> Skippable. If skipped, the player sees a summary of their build and goes to Day 1.
> If played, each stage teaches one core mechanic through a story event on a simplified map.

The palace selection was conducted by senior consorts and eunuchs. You and a group of candidates are assessed over several days. The four stages correspond to the four Confucian virtues of women (四德):

### Stage 1: 德 Virtue — The Interview

**Teaches: Dilemma choices and consequences**

Chief Eunuch Chen conducts private interviews. No dice — pure choices. This is the game's first dilemma.

*The simplified map shows one location: the Interview Chamber.*

He asks three questions. Your answers have no "right" option — they shape your character.

**Q1:** "Why did your family send you to the palace?"
- "To serve the Emperor faithfully" → Virtue +1
- "To elevate the Wu name" → (neutral)
- "I chose to come" → Resolve +1
- "Because my father's debts required a favor from the court" → (honest, Cunning +1 — Chen respects it)

**Q2:** "A senior consort gives you an order you believe is unjust. What do you do?"
- "Obey. She outranks me." → Discretion +1
- "Refuse and explain why." → Resolve +1
- "Obey, then find a way to undo the harm quietly." → Resourcefulness +1
- "Report her to the examiners." → Shadow Reach +1

**Q3:** "What do you fear most about palace life?"
- "Losing myself." → Spiritual Arts +1
- "Failing my family." → (no bonus — but Chen remembers your honesty later)
- "Nothing." → Ruthlessness +1 (he doesn't believe you, but notes the bravado)
- "Being forgotten." → Beauty +1 (he understands ambition disguised as fear)

**Mechanic taught:** Dilemmas have multiple choices. None are "wrong." All have consequences. Some consequences are visible, some are hidden.

---

### Stage 2: 言 Speech — The Poetry Recitation

**Teaches: Dice checks and stat pools**

Candidates are gathered in the library. Consort Xu presides. You must recite a poem and answer a question about its meaning.

*The simplified map shows the Library. One event appears with a dice check.*

- **Stat check:** Eloquence + Scholarship
- **Threshold:** 2 (easy — this is the tutorial)
- The player sees the dice pool form, rolls, and experiences success or failure for the first time.
- **Success:** Consort Xu nods. "Acceptable." Xu favor +1.
- **Failure:** You stumble on a line. Xu is expressionless. No penalty — but you feel it.

**Then a follow-up dilemma** (teaches that dilemmas can appear inside events):
- The candidate after you (Lady Song) freezes completely. She can't speak.
- "Whisper a hint to her" → Virtue +1, Song favor +2
- "Stay silent" → nothing
- "Recite loudly enough that she can follow your lead" → Eloquence check. Risky but if success: both of you shine.

**Mechanic taught:** Dice pools, stat checks, thresholds, success/failure. Dilemmas can appear within events.

---

### Stage 3: 容 Deportment — The Formal Presentation

**Teaches: Assigning agents to event slots**

The candidates are presented to the panel of senior consorts. But this isn't just about you — Chunhua is assessed alongside you. A maid reflects on her mistress.

*The simplified map shows the Presentation Hall. One event with TWO SLOTS: one for you (mandatory), one for Chunhua (optional but helps).*

- The player learns to assign agents to event slots.
- **Your slot:** Beauty + Discretion ≥ 2
- **Chunhua's slot (optional):** Her strongest stat is tested. If she's assigned, she adds to your pool.
- **With Chunhua:** The check is easier. You learn that agents add their stats to yours.
- **Without:** Harder but possible.

**Dilemma (after-roll):**
- Consort Yang asks you to pour tea for the panel. The seating is deliberately wrong — a lower-ranked consort is placed above a higher one.
- "Pour in seat order" → safe, invisible
- "Pour in correct rank order" → Yang smiles. "She knows protocol." Yang favor +1.

**Mechanic taught:** Assigning agents to slots. Agents contribute stats. Optional slots help but aren't required.

---

### Stage 4: 功 Skills — The Household Test

**Teaches: Map with multiple locations, timed events, resource management**

A practical test. You're given a small budget (2 silver) and one day to prepare a gift for the Emperor's upcoming birthday. The simplified map shows THREE locations: the Silk Store, the Calligraphy Shop, and the Kitchen.

Each location has an event. You can only do two of three (teaching time/resource constraints):

- **Silk Store:** Resourcefulness ≥ 2. Produce an embroidered panel. Costs 1 silver.
- **Calligraphy Shop:** Scholarship ≥ 2. Write a birthday poem on fine paper. Costs 1 silver.
- **Kitchen:** Vitality + Resourcefulness ≥ 1. Prepare a tray of seasonal sweets. Free but easy.

The quality of your gift depends on which you chose and whether you succeeded:
- Two successes: Excellent gift. Yang favor +1. Mentioned to the Emperor.
- One success: Adequate. You pass.
- Zero: You present something mediocre. No penalty — you still enter the palace.

**Mechanic taught:** Multiple events on a map. You can't do everything. Resources (silver) are scarce. Choose wisely.

### Post-Tutorial: The Assignment

Based on all your choices across character creation + tutorial:

**Starting Build Summary** (shown to the player):
- Final stats (base + background + education + tutorial bonuses)
- Starting silver
- Starting reputation

## Quick Start (Skip Tutorial)

Experienced players see:
1. Layer 1: Pick family background
2. Layer 2: Pick two educations
3. Layer 3: Pick disposition + Chunhua archetype
4. Summary screen with final stats
5. → Day 1

No tutorial events. No bonus stat tweaks from tutorial performance. Faster but you miss 0–4 stat points and the initial relationship seeds.
