# Map & Locations

> Sultan's Game equivalent: [Map / Locations](../sultans-game-reference/COMPLETE_MECHANICS.md#map--locations)

## The Palace Map

The map is presented as a **painted silk scroll** depicting the inner palace (内廷) of the Forbidden City from a bird's-eye view. Locations are illustrated buildings/courtyards that glow when events are available. The aesthetic is ink-wash painting (水墨画) with gold leaf accents.

Events appear as floating cards above their location, showing title, countdown timer, and slot requirements.

---

## Starting Locations (Always Available)

### Your Chambers (寝殿)
- **Function**: Home base. Rest, Contemplation at the Bronze Mirror, personal cultivation, intimacy events
- **Daily events**: Estate Management (silver income), Contemplation (once per day)
- **Upgradeable**: As your rank increases, your chambers improve (see [Ranks & Estates](../world/RANKS_AND_ESTATES.md))
- **Special**: Only location where you can resolve Chunhua's Resentment

### Inner Court (内廷朝会)
- **Function**: Daily political arena
- **Daily event**: Morning Court — manage alliances, counter Slander, gain/lose Imperial Favor
- **Requirements**: At least 1 noble-tagged agent must accompany you
- **Special**: When the Emperor is present (not every day), bonus opportunities for direct Favor gain

### Household Office (管事处)
- **Function**: Manage your assigned servants and estate resources
- **Daily event**: Estate Management — Cunning + Eloquence check for silver income
- **Staffing**: Can assign 1-2 agents to increase income
- **Special**: Higher rank = larger household = more base income

### Imperial Library (藏书阁)
- **Function**: Stat improvement through study
- **Daily event**: Buy scrolls (1 silver each) — permanently +1 to a stat
- **Bonus**: A random scholar or official visits daily. Win a stat check to befriend them (becomes a potential ally at court)
- **Requirements**: Agent must have Scholarship 2+ to access

## Rank-Gated Locations

### Imperial Gardens (御花园) — Rank 7+ (Jieyu)
- **Function**: Social mixing ground, intelligence gathering, chance encounters
- **Events**: Garden Stroll (Discretion + Beauty), chance meeting with the Emperor, flower arrangement contests
- **Special**: Nobles from other factions gather here — primary recruitment and alliance-building venue
- **Intelligence**: High-quality Court Whispers intelligence type

### Emperor's Quarters (龙寝/乾清宫) — By Invitation Only
- **Function**: Bedchamber visits, direct Imperial Favor gain, pregnancy chance
- **Access**: Only when the Emperor summons you (frequency scales with Imperial Favor)
- **Events**: The visit itself is an event — Beauty + Vitality check. Success = major Favor gain. Exceptional success = pregnancy chance.
- **Risk**: Other consorts learn you were summoned → jealousy events trigger

### Hall of Mental Cultivation (养心殿) — Rank 4+ (Guipin) + Imperial Favor 10+
- **Function**: The Emperor's working office. Political influence at the highest level.
- **Events**: Advise on state matters (Cunning + Scholarship), recommend appointments, influence policy
- **Special**: This is where Wu family political goals are advanced most directly

## Unlockable Locations

### Wu Family Network (外戚联络所) — Shadow Reach 3 + 10 silver to establish
- **Function**: Recruit outside agents, receive family letters, trade goods
- **Agents**: Offers 3 outside contacts (merchants, scholars, retired officials). Refreshes every 7 days.
- **Special**: Family letters create side objectives that contribute to Wu Family Standing (meta-progression)

### Palace Physician (太医院) — Unlocks when any agent is injured/poisoned
- **Function**: Heal conditions
- **Cost**: 1 silver per treatment
- **Conditions treatable**: Poisoned, Ill, Injured
- **Special**: Building a relationship with the physician (Eloquence checks over time) unlocks: poison identification, antidote access, pregnancy care bonuses

### Eunuch Quarter (内侍省) — Shadow Reach 3
- **Function**: Eunuch spy network, agent training
- **Training**: 5 silver per eunuch agent (choose stat specialization)
- **Spy network**: Generates 1 Palace Secrets intelligence per 7 days at Shadow Reach 5+
- **Special**: The Chief Eunuch is a major NPC here — his favor is separate from the Emperor's

### Buddhist Temple (佛寺/大佛堂) — Virtue 3
- **Function**: Meditation, spiritual events, sanctuary
- **Events**: Meditation (Spiritual Arts → Heavenly Sight gain), blessing requests (rare Golden Dice source), sanctuary (hide an agent from persecution for 3 days)
- **Special**: Higher Heavenly Sight unlocks inner sanctum with supernatural event chains

### Trade Quarter (市坊) — Via eunuch contact (Shadow Reach 5)
- **Function**: Black market goods — poisons, rare accessories, forged documents
- **Events**: Purchase poisons (Ruthlessness gate), rare equipment, intelligence from outside the palace
- **Risk**: Being caught shopping here = Slander + Shadow Reach loss

### Festival Grounds (庆典场) — During festivals only
- **Function**: Special seasonal mega-events (see below)
- **Temporary**: Only appears during the 4 major festivals per run
- **Events**: Festival preparation (multi-day), performances, competitions, Emperor's presence guaranteed

## Seasonal Festivals

The Tang Dynasty calendar creates 4 major festivals during a standard run. Each is a multi-day special event with unique opportunities:

| Festival | Timing | Theme | Key Opportunity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lantern Festival** (元宵节) | Early run | Light, poetry, romance | Poetry contest (Ceremony edicts), romantic encounter chance |
| **Qingming** (清明节) | Early-mid run | Ancestors, remembrance | Spiritual events (Heavenly Sight), family honor (Wu Standing) |
| **Qixi** (七夕) | Mid run | Love, the Weaver Girl myth | Emperor's attention guaranteed, Favor edicts |
| **Mid-Autumn** (中秋节) | Late run | Harvest, reunion, moon | Grand banquet (Ceremony), political maneuvering (Dominion) |

Festivals are **optional but powerful** — they create the best opportunities to break high-tier edicts, but require significant preparation (silver, agents, multi-day commitment).

## Location Upgrade System

Some locations can be improved through investment:

| Location | Upgrade | Cost | Benefit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Your Chambers | Incense burner | 3 silver | +1 to Contemplation results |
| Your Chambers | Silk screens | 5 silver | +1 Beauty for events in chambers |
| Your Chambers | Medicine cabinet | 5 silver | Self-treat minor conditions |
| Household Office | Better ledgers | 3 silver | +1 silver from estate management |
| Household Office | Servant quarters | 8 silver | +1 agent capacity |
| Wu Family Network | Carrier pigeon roost | 5 silver | Family letters arrive faster |
| Eunuch Quarter | Training grounds | 10 silver | Trained eunuchs start at higher stats |

Upgrades persist for the run. Some especially valuable ones can be "remembered" across runs via Wu Family Standing.

## Map Flow — A Typical Day

```
DAWN: New events appear on the map
  - Morning Court is always available
  - Estate Management is always available
  - 1-3 new timed/random events appear at various locations
  - Any multi-day events in progress show remaining days

MORNING: Plan and assign agents
  - Review available events and their requirements
  - Drag agent cards to event slots
  - Decide: do you attend court personally? Send Chunhua? Stay in chambers?

MIDDAY: Contemplation (optional, once per day)
  - Gaze into the Bronze Mirror with a chosen card

EVENING: All events resolve
  - Dice checks roll
  - Outcomes applied
  - New story beats trigger

NIGHT: Day ends
  - Edict timer decreases
  - Conditions tick (poison worsens, pregnancy advances)
  - Agents return from completed events
  - Multi-day events tick down
```
