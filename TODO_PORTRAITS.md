## Style Notes
- Ink-wash painting aesthetic (matching game's visual style)
- Dark backgrounds work best (cards have dark borders)
- Portraits should be head + upper chest, facing slightly left or right
- Expression should hint at personality (Yang: composed authority, Zheng: sly smile, Song: wide eyes)
- Current working SVGs are ~800KB each — similar detail level is fine
